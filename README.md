# Rosalind
Basic tools for genetic sequences

License: MIT

### Commands
- **Sequence**
    - new sequence: `new`
        - enter a new sequence
        - enter sequence optionaly with `5'-` or `3'-` at the beginning and end to indicate orientation
    - sequence: `sequence`, `seq`, `print seq`, `print sequence`
        - prints sequence
    - length: `length`, `len`
        - gives length of DNA, RNA or protein seqence in nt or aa
    - count: `count`
        - counts each nucleotide or amino acid and their portions

- **Sequence Editing**
    - reverse: `reverse`, `rev`
        - reverses DNA, RNA or protein seqence
    - complement: `complement`, `comp`
        - generates the complement sequence
        - applicable to DNA and RNA sequences
    - reverse complement: `reverse complement`, `reverse comp`, `rev complement`, `rev comp`, `revcomp`
        - generates the reverse complement sequence
    - transcribe: `transcribe`, `trc`
        - transcribes DNA to RNA sequence or retranscribes RNA to DNA sequence
    - translate: `translate`, `trl`
        - translates DNA or RNA sequence to protein sequence

- **Other**
    - undo: `undo`
    - redo: `redo`
    - help: `help`, `?`
        - refers to this file
